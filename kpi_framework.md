## KPI 1 — Monthly Revenue

Definition: Total revenue generated each month.

Formula: Sum of (quantity × unit_price) grouped by month.

Data Source: orders, order_items, products

Baseline Value: Revenue shows variation across months with some growth.

Interpretation: Helps track business performance over time and identify growth periods.

---

## KPI 2 — Revenue Growth Rate

Definition: Percentage change in revenue compared to the previous month.

Formula: (Current Month - Previous Month) / Previous Month

Data Source: Monthly revenue

Baseline Value: Growth fluctuates between months.

Interpretation: Shows whether the business is growing or declining.

---

## KPI 3 — Average Order Value (AOV)

Definition: Average amount spent per order.

Formula: Total revenue / number of orders

Data Source: orders, order_items

Baseline Value: Around 109 JOD

Interpretation: Indicates customer spending behavior.

---

## KPI 4 — Revenue by City

Definition: Total revenue generated by each city.

Data Source: customers, orders, order_items

Baseline Value: Some cities generate more revenue than others.

Interpretation: Helps identify high-performing locations.

---

## KPI 5 — Revenue by Category

Definition: Revenue generated by each product category.

Data Source: products, order_items

Baseline Value: Certain categories outperform others.

Interpretation: Shows which products drive the most revenue.
